Biohazard Tracker is an online hub for staying updated on potential threats to human health. We are currently in-development, and still have a multitude of tools to implement. As of September 25, 2020, only the COVID-19 and SARS-CoV maps have accesible map data and information.

Look to see these features in the future:

  • Symptom questionnaires
  • Risk assessment based on your demographic
  • Account registration for alerts, updates, and to save your health information
  • Inclution of health threats other than viruses, ex. natural disastors and air quality
  • More information about the imposing threats
  • External tools, such as centers dedicated to the issue and charities
  • Searchable map data for ease of access
